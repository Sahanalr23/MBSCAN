MBSCAN Automatic Milk MBRT Analyzer

MBscan is a Milk MBRT analyzer that automatically records decolorization time by digitally monitoring the reaction's endpoint. Equipped with an auto-mixing feature ensuring timely tube inversion, it delivers detailed data via our cloud dashboard and phone app, easily accessible from any location. It provides early predictions based on kinetics for both raw and pasteurized milk.

How To Use

STEPS

1

Add milk and dye to the test tube

2

Insert it in one of the channels in MBscan

3

Enter sample details and click Start

About TESTRIGHT

TestRight is a pioneer in spectroscopy. Based in Noida, we have developed India’s 1st Raman spectrometer, apart from fluorescence and UV-Vis. Using our expertise in opto-electronics, we have developed MBscan to help the dairy industry improve the overall quality of Milk.
