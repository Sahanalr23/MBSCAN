About MBSCAN

TestRight is a pioneer in spectroscopy. Based in Noida, we have developed India’s 1st Raman spectrometer, apart from fluorescence and UV-Vis. Using our expertise in opto-electronics, we have developed MBscan to help the dairy industry improve the overall quality of Milk. We are further working on to reduce the MBRT measurement time of pasteurised milk from 6hrs to 40 min and are piloting with dairy giants in Delhi.
